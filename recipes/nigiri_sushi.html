Nigiri Sushi

Blue Fin Tuna Nigiri Sushi

Ingredients

  • High Quality Sashimi Grade Blue Fin Tuna
  • Cooked Sushi Rice
  • Freshly Grinded Wasabi paste

Steps

  1. Carefully take a slice of cut Blue Fin Tuna Sashimi
  2. With your other hand, grab an appropriate amount of Sushi Rice
  3. Apply slight pressure to mold the rice into shape
  4. Apply an appropriate amount of Wasabi paste on the bottom of the Sashmi
  5. Put the Sashmi on the rice and apply light pressure to mold them together
